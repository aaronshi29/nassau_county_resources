Welcome to the Community Living Resources Map

This portal is designed to help people quickly find community resources such as shelters, food support, medical care, legal services, hygiene/basic-needs support, and crisis resources.

Disclaimer: Resource details can change. Please verify hours, eligibility, and availability directly with the provider before visiting.

Context of this work

This map supports public access to essential living resources. It is intended to make local help easier to discover and understand through a clear, searchable map interface.

How to use this map

Use the search bar, turn categories on or off with the layer controls, click markers for details, and use “Show all visible” to zoom to the filtered results.

Where to look for help

For emergencies call 911. For urgent mental health support in the U.S., call or text 988. You can also use the resource panel to find nearby organizations.

How to contribute

Community members can suggest updates, corrections, and new locations through a form, email address, or GitHub issue link added by the project maintainer.
